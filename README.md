## Overview
Interview Bot is a lightweight Streamlit application that simulates realistic job interviews.  
Enter your background, choose the company & position level, and chat with an AI interviewer.  
After five exchanges you receive an instant performance score and actionable feedback.

## Features
- **Role-specific interviews** – Data Scientist, ML Engineer, BI Analyst, etc.  
- **Configurable seniority** – Junior → Senior.  
- **Company presets** – Amazon, Meta, Spotify, Nestlé, LinkedIn, Udemy, 365 Company.  
- **GPT-4o powered** – Natural, context-aware questions.  
- **Instant feedback** – 1-10 score + concise review.

Usage Tips

    Keep answers concise (≤ 1 000 chars) for best results.
    Click “Restart Interview” at any time to reset the session.
    All data stays local; only the conversation is sent to OpenAI for questions/feedback.
